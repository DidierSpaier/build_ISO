wpa_supplicant-nogui: WPA2 (IEEE 802.11i / RSN). Supplicant is the IEEE 802.1X/WPA
wpa_supplicant-nogui: component that is used in the client stations. It implements key
wpa_supplicant-nogui: negotiation with a WPA Authenticator and it controls the roaming and
wpa_supplicant-nogui: IEEE 802.11 authentication/association of the wlan driver.
